Hi Jennifer, 

I haven’t been looking for a HEDIS job, as the season is between Oct- March. The program was worth it for me. I’m searching for a remote Chronic Care Manager or Remote Patient Monitoring position. I have a couple interviews next week. My resume was redone for me (part of the cost of the course) and it’s getting attention. Marie has a jobs page when you finish the course, and daily adds remote jobs. She has just started offering a HEDIS only course that has a lower cost if that’s the only part you’re interested in. Over 300 of her nurses from the course were hired for HEDIS positions. I think it definitely is worth it and gives you an edge when hiring season comes. She’s made many connections and companies want to hire her nurses. 
 

Wishing you the best, 

Rhonda
